Production Samples

Can't Stay - Daniel Rector

cantstay.mp3

I recorded this track in the summer of 2014 at an impromptu session in Roanoke, VA. The recording was done all in one take, with less than 8 tracks and only 3 musicians. The room was a large warehouse, and the copious amounts of empty space were used for reverb. The final sound was achieved with minimul use of compression and eq.

Fix You - Coldplay (Joseph Tormey Bootleg)

fixyou.mp3

This project was mastered by myself for digital distrubution. I used minimal band compression and tasteful limiting to push the track hard without clipping or distortion.

Push - JP Ford

push.mp3

I recorded the vocalist for this project over an instrumental he provided. I used stacking techniques with creative compression to bring out his unique voice. I was able to blend the track together with more compression/eq and create a polished track.
